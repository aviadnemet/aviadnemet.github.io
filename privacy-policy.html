Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy applies to Android applications developed and published by Aviad Nemet, including English for the Psychometric Exam ("אנגלית לפסיכומטרי").

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All study progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for displaying ads)

Please refer to the privacy policies of these services for details on what data they may collect.

Ads

The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences through your device's Google settings.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
