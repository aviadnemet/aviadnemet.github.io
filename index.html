App

אנגלית לפסיכומטרי
English for the Psychometric Exam

A vocabulary training app for Israeli students preparing for the English section of the Psychometric exam. Practice English–Hebrew word pairs and build lasting knowledge through evidence-based study methods.

  • Vocabulary training with English–Hebrew word pairs
  • Spaced repetition for long-term retention
  • Smart flashcards
  • Progress tracking
  • Quiz-based and gamified learning

Built to give students a focused, no-nonsense tool for the vocabulary section of the exam.
